# Ride-Hub
# 🚴 Sales & Customer Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing customer behavior, product performance, and sales distribution in a retail cycling business.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Understand customer demographics
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Analyze sales distribution across regions
- Discover opportunities for business growth

## 📊 Key Insights
- Bikes contribute ~96% of total sales
- Road & Mountain Bikes generate over 80% of revenue
- Age group 40–49 is the most active customer segment
- 37% repeat customers → opportunity to improve retention
- Accessories & Clothing categories are underutilized
- Sales concentrated in US & Australia, with growth potential in other markets

## 📈 Business Recommendations
- Improve cross-selling (Accessories & Clothing)
- Introduce bundling strategies
- Enhance customer retention programs
- Expand in underperforming regions
